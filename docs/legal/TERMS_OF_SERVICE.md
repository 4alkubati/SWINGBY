## 3. The SwingBy Marketplace

### 3.1 What we are
SwingBy is a **marketplace**. We connect clients with independent businesses. SwingBy does not perform the underlying services and is not party to the agreement between client and business.

### 3.2 What we are not
- We are not your employer (if you're a business or employee)
- We are not a guarantor of work quality
- We are not an escrow agent in the legal sense — we facilitate payment timing but the contractual relationship is between you and the other party

### 3.3 Quote and booking flow
1. Client posts a job with date, description, and (optionally) photos
2. Businesses send quotes (price only — no message attached to the quote itself)
3. A chat thread opens on that quote — the client and the quoting business can message each other while the post is still open
4. Client picks a quote → a Booking is created → the existing quote conversation carries over onto the booking
5. Worker arrives, completes the job, may upload proof photos, and marks it complete
6. Payment release: 50% on confirmation, 50% (minus 10% platform fee) on completion
7. Client reviews the worker

## 4. Payments, Fees, and Cancellations

### 4.1 Platform fee
SwingBy charges the **business** a **10% platform fee** on the gross job amount. Clients pay the full quoted price.

### 4.2 Payment timing (escrow)
- **On booking confirmation:** 50% released to business, 50% held in escrow
- **On job completion:** remaining 50% minus 10% fee released to business
- **Total to business:** 90% of quoted price
- Disputed jobs hold the unreleased balance until resolved (§7). Note the 50% released on confirmation has already been paid out; recovering it is a support decision, not an automatic reversal.

### 4.3 Cancellations (by client)
| When cancellation occurs | Penalty to client |
|---|---|
| More than 48 hours before scheduled job | 25% of quoted price |
| 48 hours or less before scheduled job | 50% of quoted price |
| After worker arrives | 100% of quoted price |

Penalties are paid to the business as compensation for held time.

### 4.4 Cancellations (by business)
A business cancelling within 24 hours of the scheduled job forfeits the booking and may receive a profile demerit. Repeated late cancellations may result in account suspension.
