# Drip: Reactivation Sequence (3 emails for users inactive 60+ days)

> Goal: Re-engage users who haven't posted a job or accepted a booking in 60+ days.
> Trigger: No activity (job posted, booking made, or login) in 60 days.
> Voice: Low-pressure, honest. Not desperate. Give them a reason to come back, not a guilt trip.
> CASL: Users have previously opted in. Each email includes unsubscribe link.

---

## Email 1 — Day 0 of reactivation sequence (60 days inactive)

**Subject:** It's been a while, {{first_name}}

**Preview text:** SwingBy has changed. Here's what's new in Calgary.

---

Hi {{first_name}},

We noticed you haven't been on SwingBy in a while. That's okay — life gets busy.

**Browse local services:** [swingby.ca](https://swingby.ca)

And if SwingBy just isn't the right fit right now, no hard feelings. You can unsubscribe below.

— The SwingBy team
