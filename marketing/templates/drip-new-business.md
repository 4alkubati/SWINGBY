## Email 2 — Day 2 (profile check)

**Subject:** One tip to get more quote requests

**Preview text:** The single biggest factor in getting booked on SwingBy.

---

Hi {{first_name}},

The businesses on SwingBy that get the most bookings have one thing in common: they respond to quote requests within 2 hours.

Clients post a job, get several quotes, and book the first good one. If you respond 24 hours later, the job is usually gone.

**Tips for fast responses:**

- Enable push notifications on the SwingBy app (coming soon) or email notifications in your settings
- Set your service availability so you only receive requests when you're actually available
- Keep a short quote template ready for your most common job types

If you haven't enabled email notifications for new job requests yet, do it now: [Account settings](https://swingby.ca/app/settings/notifications)

**Subject:** Have you sent your first quote yet?

**Preview text:** New Calgary clients are posting jobs in your category this week.

---

Hi {{first_name}},

Clients in your service area are posting jobs right now. Here's how to find them and send your first quote:

1. Go to your [SwingBy dashboard](https://swingby.ca/app/dashboard)
2. Check the "Available jobs" section for new posts in your category
3. Click any job to view the details and send a quote

When quoting, be specific: name your price, confirm your availability, and mention one relevant detail about your experience with this type of job. Clients respond to specificity.

First quote tip: price competitively for your first few jobs to build your verified review base. A 4.9-star rating with 5 reviews is worth more than a 5.0 with zero.

## Email 5 — Day 14 (growth)

**Subject:** Growing your SwingBy business

**Preview text:** Tips for turning one-time clients into regulars.

---

Hi {{first_name}},

Two weeks in. Here's what the busiest businesses on SwingBy do consistently:

**1. Ask for reviews.** After every job, message the client through SwingBy and ask them to leave a review. Most clients are happy to, they just need the reminder.

**2. Set service availability accurately.** Only quote on jobs you can actually complete this week. A declined job or a late cancellation hurts your rating.

**3. Price transparently.** Clients who understand why a job costs what it does are less likely to push back on the quote. Include a one-line explanation in your quotes.

**4. Save a job description template.** Your most common job type probably comes up 3-4x per week. Write a standard quote response you can personalize in 30 seconds.

Any questions? Reply to this email. We read every one.
