# 10 — Partnerships

> Who we want as allies, what we offer, what we ask for.

---

## Why partnerships matter for a marketplace

A signed-on partner can deliver 100 businesses or 10,000 clients overnight — the same as 6 months of cold outreach. They also lend credibility, which a 2-person startup cannot buy.

But partnerships only work if both sides win. Lead with what we give, not what we ask.

---

## Tier 1 — High-leverage partners (target by month 3)

### 1. Calgary Chamber of Commerce
- **Who they reach:** 1,800+ Calgary businesses
- **What we offer:** Free 1-year Featured listing + Verified badge for any Chamber member who signs up. Sponsored event ("Local Services Day").
- **What we ask:** Inclusion in their member directory under "Tech partners," 1 email blast to members about SwingBy, founder speaking slot at a monthly mixer.
- **Contact:** Membership team via chamber website.

### 2. Calgary Economic Development (CED)
- **Who they reach:** Startup ecosystem, media, investors
- **What we offer:** Free for first 200 businesses in CED-promoted programs; data on Calgary service economy.
- **What we ask:** Inclusion in their "Made in Calgary" startup showcase, intro to local investors, press relations.
- **Contact:** Their tech ecosystem lead.

### 3. SAIT / Bow Valley College / Olds College
- **Who they reach:** Trade-school grads (electricians, plumbers, carpenters, cosmetologists) — exactly our ICP entering the workforce
- **What we offer:** Free 1-year SwingBy account for graduating students + employer-style "first 5 bookings free" promo.
- **What we ask:** Inclusion in their career-services package, 1 in-person info session per semester, table at career fair.
- **Contact:** Career services office of each.

### 4. The City of Calgary — Open for Business
- **Who they reach:** Brand-new businesses just incorporated
- **What we offer:** Featured in their new-business welcome packet; free Verified badge year 1.
- **What we ask:** Logo + intro in their "tools for new businesses" page.

## Tier 2 — Aligned product/services (target by month 4)

### 5. Squarespace / Wix / WordPress agencies
- Many service businesses use these for websites
- **Offer:** "Embed your SwingBy booking widget on your site"
- **Ask:** Agency promotes SwingBy to their service-business clients (rev share TBD)

### 6. Stripe / Square / payment processors
- **Offer:** Co-marketing case study
- **Ask:** Inclusion in their "made with Stripe" or partner directory

### 7. QuickBooks / Wave / FreshBooks
- Our businesses do bookkeeping in these
- **Offer:** Build an integration that exports SwingBy bookings to their accounting tool
- **Ask:** Listing in their app marketplace, co-marketing

### 8. Calgary trade-specific associations
- Calgary Construction Association, Alberta Cleaners' Network, Pet Industry Joint Advisory Council, etc.
- **Offer:** Exclusive member discount (3% take rate for 6 months instead of 5%)
- **Ask:** Newsletter blast, member directory listing

---

## Tier 3 — Influencer & creator partnerships (target by month 2)

Calgary lifestyle micro-influencers (5-50k followers). Real reach > big numbers in this segment.

### Profiles to approach
- Calgary lifestyle creators (food, home, dog people)
- Trade content creators (handyman TikTok, cleaning TikTok, etc.)
- Calgary realtors with strong social (they refer service providers daily)

### Offer
- $100-500 product fee per post depending on reach
- Free SwingBy services up to $500 for them personally
- Affiliate code (referrer gets $25, referee gets $15 off first booking)

### Ask
- 2 IG posts + 2 stories + 1 reel over 30 days, OR
- 3 TikToks, OR
- 1 dedicated YouTube video

### Sourcing
Reach out via DM. Keep pitch under 5 sentences. Always be willing to walk away.

## Partnership playbook

### Cold-pitch template

> Subject: [Partner name] + SwingBy — 15 min?
> 
> Hi [name],
> 
> I'm Amr — founder of SwingBy, a Calgary-based booking marketplace for local services (launched [date]).
> 
> We have [X businesses, Y bookings, Z reviews] in Calgary and we're growing [X]% MoM.
> 
> I think there's a fit with [partner org]. We can offer [specific value to them]. In return, we'd love [specific ask].
> 
> 15 min next week to explore?
> 
> Amr

### Negotiation rules
- Always offer first, ask second
- Cap rev shares at 20% for affiliates, 30% for embed-style integrations
- Never sign exclusivity for first 18 months
- Always include a 90-day out clause

### After signing
- Create dedicated landing page: `/partners/[partner-slug]`
- Track partner-attributed signups in PostHog (utm_source=partner_[slug])
- Monthly check-in with the partner
- Quarterly co-marketing push

---

## What we don't do

- ❌ Pay slotting fees to be listed somewhere
- ❌ Exclusive deals (they kill optionality)
- ❌ White-label SwingBy under a partner's brand (we'd be building someone else's marketplace)
- ❌ Custom contracts in year 1 (we don't have the leverage or the time)
