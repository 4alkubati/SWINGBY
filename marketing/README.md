# SwingBy Marketing & Monetization

This folder is the **single source of truth** for everything SwingBy needs to go from "the product is built" to "people are paying us."

It is owned by the founder (Amr) and is updated at the end of every marketing/business sprint.

## How to use this folder

1. **Before any marketing decision** — read the relevant file. Don't reinvent.
2. **After any marketing experiment** — update the relevant file with what worked and what didn't.
3. **When asked "how do we make money?"** — open `01-monetization-strategy.md`. That's the answer.
