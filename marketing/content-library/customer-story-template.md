# Customer Story Template

Use this fill-in-the-blank format to turn real client/business experiences into shareable content. Edit freely — the goal is an authentic voice, not marketing speak.

---

## Fill-in-the-blank format

**Headline:**
> [Name], a [client/business owner] in [neighbourhood], [result achieved] using SwingBy.

**The before:**
> Before SwingBy, [Name] used to [old painful process]. It would take [time/effort] and they [specific frustration].

**The moment:**
> They [found SwingBy / posted their first job / signed up as a business] on [date or timeframe].

**What happened:**
> Within [time], [specific outcome]. [Quote from person if available].

**The after:**
> Now, [Name] [current positive state]. [Specific measurable result if possible].

**Call to action:**
> Ready to [same result]? [CTA: Post a job free / List your business]

## Tips for using customer stories

- **Always get permission** before using a real person's name or photo. A quick "can I share your experience?" is enough.
- **Specifics win.** "Booked in 4 hours" beats "booked quickly."
- **Keep the voice theirs.** Light editing for clarity is fine. Don't polish out the personality.
- **Match the platform:** IG = cut to 3 sentences + CTA. LinkedIn = the full story. Email = use in testimonial section.
