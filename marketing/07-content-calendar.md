# 07 — 90-Day Content Calendar

> Week-by-week content plan across blog, social, and email.

---

## Operating principles

1. **One pillar per week.** Pick a theme each Monday. Everything that week ties back.
2. **Repurpose ruthlessly.** One blog post = 3 Instagram posts = 1 TikTok = 1 LinkedIn = 1 email.
3. **Pull from real events.** Customer wins, founder learnings, neighbourhood spotlights. Avoid evergreen filler.
4. **Schedule, don't post live.** Use n8n (see `11-n8n-social-workflow.md`) or Buffer. Frees ~5 hrs/wk.

---

## Cadence summary

| Channel | Frequency | Format |
|---|---|---|
| Blog | 1 long-form/wk | 800-1500 words |
| Instagram feed | 4 posts/wk | 1 carousel, 2 single image, 1 reel |
| Instagram stories | 8/wk | Polls, behind-the-scenes, customer DMs |
| TikTok | 3 videos/wk | 15-30s, captioned |
| LinkedIn | 2 posts/wk | Founder personal + company page |
| Twitter/X | 5 posts/wk | Threads, takes, replies |
| Email | 1 broadcast/wk to waitlist | + drip on signup |

## Month 1 — Launch & Calgary credibility

**Theme:** "We're live, here's why."

### Week 1 — Launch week
- **Blog:** "Why I built SwingBy" (founder story, 1200 words)
- **Instagram:** Launch carousel (5 slides) + day-in-the-life reel of first booking
- **TikTok:** "I built an app to fix [Calgary problem]" (founder talking head)
- **LinkedIn:** Launch post (founder + company)
- **Twitter:** Launch thread (10 tweets, story format)
- **Email:** "We're live" → waitlist

### Week 2 — Trust & escrow
- **Blog:** "How SwingBy keeps your money safe" (escrow explainer, 800 words)
- **Instagram:** Carousel: "5 ways to spot a scam service" — soft sell to SwingBy at end
- **TikTok:** Demo of escrow flow — "Watch what happens when a business no-shows"
- **LinkedIn:** "What I learned in our first week" (real numbers)
- **Twitter:** Thread on marketplace trust mechanisms

### Week 3 — Calgary spotlight
- **Blog:** "The 10 most-booked services in Beltline this month"
- **Instagram:** Business spotlight reel (3 businesses, 15s each)
- **TikTok:** "A day in the life of a Calgary house cleaner" (UGC with a SwingBy biz)
- **LinkedIn:** "Why we're staying Calgary-only for year 1" (controversial take = engagement)
- **Twitter:** "Underrated Calgary neighbourhoods for [service]" thread

### Week 4 — First wins recap
- **Blog:** "Our first 100 bookings — what we learned"
- **Instagram:** Numbers carousel + customer testimonial graphic
- **TikTok:** Customer wins compilation
- **LinkedIn:** Founder 30-day recap (data-heavy)
- **Twitter:** Lessons-learned thread

## Month 2 — Education & SEO

**Theme:** "Helpful answers to questions Calgary people Google."

Every week pick 2 long-tail queries. Write a blog post for each. Repurpose.

### Example weekly slate
- **Week 5 — "How much does house cleaning cost in Calgary in 2026?"**
  - Blog: pricing breakdown by home size, with real SwingBy data
  - Instagram: pricing carousel
  - TikTok: "POV: you're about to overpay for cleaning"
  - LinkedIn: pricing transparency take
  - Twitter: 1-tweet pricing summary

- **Week 6 — "What to look for when hiring a handyman in Calgary"**
- **Week 7 — "Dog walker prices and what's normal in Calgary"**
- **Week 8 — "Snow removal Calgary: when to DIY vs hire"**

Each post targets a real search term with 100-500 monthly volume. By month 6, these compound into 30-50% of client signups.

## Month 3 — Social proof & momentum

**Theme:** "People are using this and loving it."

### Weekly content sources
- **Mondays:** Customer of the week (interview them on the weekend)
- **Wednesdays:** Business of the week (founder profile mini-series)
- **Fridays:** Numbers Friday (what we shipped, jobs booked, MRR)

### Specific posts
- "Meet [Business name] — Calgary's top-rated [category]" (every week)
- "What [client name] said about us" (testimonial graphics, every Friday)
- "Behind the screens" — founder builds-in-public series on LinkedIn
- "From post to booked in 3 minutes" — UGC reels

### Email drip (starts week 9)
- Day 0: Welcome + how to post your first job
- Day 2: "Here's how to find the right business"
- Day 5: "What our escrow protects you from"
- Day 14: First reminder if no booking — "Here are 3 businesses near you"
- Day 30: Reactivation if no booking — incentive ($10 off)

---

## Content production workflow

1. **Sunday night:** founder picks the week's theme (15 min).
2. **Monday AM:** write the blog post draft (90 min).
3. **Monday PM:** edit + publish (45 min).
4. **Tuesday AM:** convert blog → 4 IG posts + 1 reel + 1 LI + 5 tweets using ChatGPT/Claude template prompts.
5. **Tuesday PM:** create visuals in Canva (1 hr) and shoot any video (45 min).
6. **Wednesday AM:** schedule everything in Buffer or n8n for the week.
7. **Daily:** respond to comments/DMs within 2 hours.

**Total weekly time:** ~6 hours. Doable while still doing customer dev + product.

## Content stockpile (write once, reuse forever)

These should live as drafts in Notion / Obsidian, ready to post anytime:
- 20 single-sentence quotes from founder (LinkedIn / Twitter filler)
- 10 customer testimonial graphic templates (just swap photo + text)
- 5 "how it works" 30s video templates (just swap voiceover)
- 30 service-category FAQs (one per category, refresh quarterly)
- 10 evergreen blog posts ("how to choose a [service]")
